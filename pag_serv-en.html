Carbon Footprint Calculation
Carbon Footprint
• Calculates greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions, expressed in units of CO2e mass.
• Includes mitigation measures, emissions reduction calculations, economic indicators, and abatement curves.
• Includes the proposal of an emissions monitoring plan and mitigation initiatives.
• Assists in the registration and recognition process at the Peruvian Carbon Footprint Registry (HC Perú) of the Ministry of the Environment (MINAM).
• Certifies service quality and commits to answering third-party inquiries.
• Includes a report and infographic.
Water Footprint
• Calculates total water resource consumption, expressed in volume of freshwater.
• Includes measures to reduce water consumption, economic indicators, and abatement curves.
• Assists in obtaining the Blue Certificate from the National Water Authority (ANA) (water footprint report and proposed design of reduction and shared value projects).
• Certifies service quality and commits to answering third-party inquiries.
• Includes a report and infographic.
Ecological Footprint
• Calculates the demand for resources taken from the planet, expressed in units of land area.
• Includes land calculation per activity: carbon sequestration, livestock, agriculture, fishing, urbanization, forest use, and waste disposal.
• Proposes measures to improve resource efficiency.
• Includes a report and infographic.
Environmental Footprint
• Life cycle analysis and multi-criteria indicators.
• Includes calculation of:
   > Greenhouse gas emissions.
   > Water resource consumption.
   > Local pollutant emissions.
   > Substances depleting the ozone layer emissions.
 • Includes a report and infographic.
Climate Change Management
• National Greenhouse Gas Inventories (INGEI)
• Mitigation measures, their economic indicators, and abatement curve.
• Monitoring and Verification Plans for mitigation initiatives.
• Design and development of Nationally Appropriate Mitigation Actions (NAMA) for each country.
• Carbon credit generation projects in VCS program and Gold Standard.
• Mitigation projects contributing to Nationally Determined Contributions (NDC).
